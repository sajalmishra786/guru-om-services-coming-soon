Our Short Story

Established in 2016, Guru Om Services is a professionally managed and rapidly expanding enterprise delivering integrated solutions across the infrastructure, industrial, and construction sectors. With expertise in construction material and metal scrap trading, bulk material supply, import and export, fabrication work, recycling operations, and skilled manpower supply, we provide comprehensive support that enables seamless project execution for our clients

Over the years, we have built strong and lasting partnerships with some of India’s most reputed organizations, including Steel Authority of India Ltd, Jindal Pipes Ltd, Ircon International Ltd, Siemens Ltd, NCC Ltd, Simplex Infrastructure Limited, KEC International Ltd, Indian Oil Corporation Limited, Tata Steel Limited, L&T Limited, Sudhir Power, M3M, and Ahluwalia Contract India Limited. Our ability to consistently meet stringent quality standards, adhere to timelines, and ensure competitive pricing has earned us the trust of industry leaders across diverse domains.
